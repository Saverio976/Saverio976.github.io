## Disable Windows Animation impacting performance

  1. Press `Windows` + `r`.
  2. Type `systempropertiesperformance` and Press `Enter`.
  3. Search for `Adjust for best performance` and Click.
  4. Optionally, Click on the options you want to keep.
  5. Click `OK`.
